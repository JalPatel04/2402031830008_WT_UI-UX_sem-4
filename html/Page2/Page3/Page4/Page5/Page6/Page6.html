About

Chetan Prakash Bhagat (born 22 April 1974) is an Indian author, columnist, screenwriter, and You Tuber. He was named one of Time magazine's 100 Most Influential People in the World in 2010.

Achievements

  1. Chetan Bhagat was recognized by IIM Ahmadabad as "The best outgoing student" in 1997.
  2. Chetan won the Society Young Achiever's Award in 2000.
  3. He won the Publisher's Recognition Award in 2005.
  4. Featured on Time magazine's list of World's 100 Most Influential People of 2010 in the Artists category.
